Vision & Mission

What Drives Us

🌍

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to quality mental health support—regardless of their location, income, or circumstances. We believe mental wellness should be a right, not a privilege.

🎓

Our Mission

To educate the world about mental health through engaging, AI-powered experiences. We spread psychoeducation and make therapeutic techniques accessible and understandable for everyone.

Our Approach

Integrative Therapy

We believe no single therapeutic approach works for everyone. That's why ai.therapy draws from multiple evidence-based modalities, allowing each AI therapist to use the techniques that resonate most with you.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT) Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) Schema Therapy Psychodynamic Therapy Gestalt Therapy Mindfulness-Based (MBCT) Humanistic Therapy Somatic Therapy Compassion-Focused Therapy
